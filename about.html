Our Story

Building World-Class
Education Standard Since 2024

Topfolex British International School was established with a bold vision: to redefine quality education in Ogun State by blending the best of British-influenced academics, cutting-edge technology, and unwavering character formation. Within just one year, we grew exponentially — a testament to the trust parents place in our system.

Located at Kajola Road, Custom Base, Ajegunle, we offer seamless learning from Crèche to Senior Secondary. Our first-ever BECE cohort achieved outstanding results with multiple A grades, and our investment in 24/7 solar power, computer labs, projectors, coding & robotics has set a new standard for modern schooling.
